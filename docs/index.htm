Careflow
https://apps.apple.com/gb/app/careflow/id536102847
Careflow is a clinical communication platform for health and social care teams that enables them to more effectively coordinate patient care.

It provides secure messaging, task management, patient list management, patient handovers, opinion and patient referral workflow and escalation of patient events through team based and individual push notifications.
Vitals Nurse & Doctor
Available via private B2B custom App Store only
  Two iOS medical apps which implementation started in 2009 and lasted for next 14 years.

Vitals Nurse iOS application is used by clinical staff to record patient observations and assessments. The application automatically calculates the Early Warning Score (EWS) and will recommend the appropriate escalation.

Vitals Doctor application is used by clinical staff to view patient observations and escalations. The application must be used on ward rounds to view patient data, including pathology results and radiology reports.
